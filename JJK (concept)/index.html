In a world haunted by Curses — cruel monsters born from negative human emotions — humanity is secretly protected by Jujutsu Sorcerers. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student with superhuman strength whose life is turned upside down when he swallows a cursed finger to save his friends from an attack.

The artifact belonged to Ryomen Sukuna, the dreaded "King of Curses." By becoming the monster's vessel, Itadori is sentenced to death. However, under the guidance of the powerful sorcerer Satoru Gojo, his execution is postponed on one condition: find and consume all of Sukuna's remaining fingers, and ultimately be executed to eradicate the curse forever.

Created by Gege Akutami and launched in 2018 in Weekly Shōnen Jump, Jujutsu Kaisen stands out for its dark tone, strategic battles, and willingness to subvert shōnen clichés. Fueled by its massive anime adaptation, the manga became a global phenomenon, surpassing 90 million copies sold before concluding in September 2024.

Produced by MAPPA, the anime adaptation of Jujutsu Kaisen premiered in 2020 and quickly became a cultural phenomenon. Renowned for its fluid animation, intense fight sequences, and faithful adaptation, the series redefined the expectations for action anime and captivated audiences worldwide.

A world where curses lurk in the shadows, sorcerers fight to protect humanity, and the line between life and death is written in cursed energy.
